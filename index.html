About

Hi, I'm Rohan.
I'm here to make you look incredible.

I'm a videographer and photographer based in Cambridge, MA, and I've spent the last eight-plus years helping real estate agents look like the best version of themselves on camera. Hundreds of properties. Hundreds of agents. And every single project is built around the person, not a template.

I got into this through music and audio production — I worked as a sound engineer, music video producer, and in-house videographer before going full-time with real estate media. That background shapes everything I do, from the rhythm of an edit to the mood of a shot.

But here's the thing I care about most: I want every agent I work with to feel genuinely confident in the content they're putting out. Most people are nervous on camera — that's normal. I don't do scripted lines or awkward poses. I just create a comfortable space where your real personality comes through, and the final product always makes you feel like you nailed it.

These days, I'm focused on one thing: helping ambitious agents and teams show up in a way that matches the level they're actually operating at — or the level they're growing into. If you care about your brand, I want to help you build it.

I'm always up for a coffee and a conversation — reach out anytime.
